Short Story

How many times did you receive those emails saying "...will not move forward" from recruiters? Being a job seeker myself, I face many challenges during job search. Let's list a few of them:

  • lack of preparation;
  • absence of a clear career objective and plan;
  • no centralized job application tracking system;
  • no centralized career tracking system;
  • etc.

The list can expand to several other challenges. Move Forward, as you have guessed, is here to help you succeed in your job applications and career in general. It’s a professional software for myself and all the other professionals or job seekers out there, looking for a system to organize their career.

Checkout the project on github. If you have some cool ideas, feel free to create issues.

Features

Job Application Tracking

The first way Move Forward tries to help is by providing a centralized job application system where you can keep track of all the jobs you've applied to or are interested in.

Career Objective Planning

Save a clear career goal and set minimal objectives you can frequently track and assess. This can help you stay motivated and driven as you'll have a clear view of what the next move should be.

Custom Career Support

Your profile and activity across the app, plus your job application records, will help assess your qualification and provide recommendations for different jobs.
